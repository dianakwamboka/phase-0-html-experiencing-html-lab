Prime ministers of New Zealand

This is a list of the prime ministers of New Zealand and the term/year each served in office upto the year 2017

  3. Dame Jenny Shipley. (1997–1999) 2 February 1952.
  4. Helen Clark. (1999–2008) 26 February 1950.
  5. Sir John Key. (2008–2016)
  6. Sir Bill English. (2016–2017)
